About ThinkPlus

ThinkPlus is an EdTech initiative focused on providing result-oriented coaching, live sessions, and mentor-led career guidance to students and professionals preparing for competitive exams and job interviews. Our mission is to make learning accessible, effective, and engaging.

Our Mission

To empower learners with world-class mentors, practical content, and continuous evaluation to help them reach their career goals.

Our Approach

  • Live interactive sessions with expert mentors
  • Personalized feedback and mock assessments
  • Practical interview & soft skill modules
